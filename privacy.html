Tong quat: Du Hoc Trung Quoc la ung dung thong tin doc lap. Chung toi TON TRONG quyen rieng tu cua ban. Thong tin ca nhan chi luu tren thiet bi cua ban, KHONG gui len may chu.

1. Thong tin chung toi thu thap

Ung dung co the luu tru cac thong tin sau tren thiet bi cua ban (chi tren dien thoai, khong gui len may chu):

  • Thong tin hoc van: GPA, trinh do HSK, bac hoc muc tieu - dung cho tinh nang so khop truong
  • So thich: Chuyen nganh, thanh pho, ngan sach - dung cho goi y truong phu hop
  • Truong yeu thich: Danh sach truong ban da luu
  • Trang thai mua hang: Cac san pham da mua (quan ly boi Apple)

2. Cach chung toi su dung thong tin

  • Tinh nang so khop truong dai hoc
  • Goi y hoc bong phu hop
  • Luu trang thai su dung app

3. Chia se thong tin

Chung toi KHONG chia se, ban hoac chuyen thong tin ca nhan cua ban cho bat ky ben thu ba nao.

4. Thanh toan

Tat ca giao dich mua hang duoc xu ly boi Apple thong qua App Store. Chung toi khong truc tiep thu thap hoac luu tru thong tin thanh toan cua ban.

5. Quyen cua ban

  • Ban co the xoa tat ca du lieu bang cach xoa ung dung
  • Ban co the chinh sua thong tin ca nhan bat ky luc nao trong tab "Ca nhan"
  • Ban co the khoi phuc giao dich mua hang qua Apple

6. Bao mat tre em
